OBSTETRICS

Obstetrics is the field of medical study concentrated

on pregnancy, childbirth and the postpartum

period.

As a medical speciality, Obstetrics is

combined with Gynaecology under the

discipline known as Obstetrics and Gynaecology (OB/GYN),

which is a surgical field.
