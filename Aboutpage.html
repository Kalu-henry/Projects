ABOUT ME

Hello there, My name is Ikechukwu Michael Kalu.

I am from Arochukwu local government in Abia State, Nigeria.

I am 26 years old, fair in complexion, and 6 foot tall.

I am the first child amongst the six children of my parents.

My position in the family is one of the reasons I am so goal-driven,

focused and determined to reach my goals.

I love playing video games, chess and watching movies of all genres.

This man is one the major reasons I decided to study medicine.

I always loved reading his books, and the fact that he got
to this point

of being recognised worldwide, I so thought that I could also achieve such

great feat with hardwork, resilience, determination and focus.

I am a gamer, but I developed special love for chess in my first year as a medical student.

A few of my friends as at that time introduced the game to me, of course I was a fish at that time.

But the reverse is the case now, I am not a chess Grand Master like Magnus Carlsen, but I can assure you I am a tough nut to crack.

Right from my secondary school days, I have been very good in this sport.

I proved my worth once more in the university amongst my dear friends.

Playing with them was fun, but winning was more fun.

Another thing I love doing other than reading, playing chess, table tennis and watching movies, is playing video games. Preferrably fifa.

This helps me calm down and ease of the stress of the day. This is one of the many things I can beat my chest and say that I am good at.
